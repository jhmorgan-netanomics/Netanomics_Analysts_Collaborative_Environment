References: Fitting
 
 
Graybill, Franklin A. and Iyer, Hariharan K. (1994)
Regression Analysis: Concepts and Applications.
Duxbury Press, Belmont, Califronia.
(Beautifully written; includes SAS & MINITAB code, insightful
client-statistician dialogues, & concrete author recommendations)
 
Draper, N. R. and Smith, H. (1968).
Applied Regression Analysis.
Wiley, New York.
(The classic)
 
Neter, John, Wasserman, William, and Kutner, Michael H. (1985).
Applied Linear Statistical Models: Regression, Analysis
of Variance, and Experimental Designs. Irwin, Homewood, Illinois.
Well-written; good diagnostics, good comments on corrective action.
 
Hamilton, Lawrence C. (1992).
Regression with Graphics: A Second Course in
Applied Statistics.
Brooks/Cole Publishing, Pacific Grove, California.
(copious graphics)
