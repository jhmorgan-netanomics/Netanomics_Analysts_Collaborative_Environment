----------  *JAPANESE W'S*  ----------

The 5 W's (and 1 H) which one should ask for each component
in the work process (so as to improve work effeiciency)--

   1. Why

   2. What

   3. Where

   4. Who

   5. When

   6. How


   Source--Total Quality Control (TQC): The Japanese Way.
           Q. C. Trends, ASugust 1985, page 9.
