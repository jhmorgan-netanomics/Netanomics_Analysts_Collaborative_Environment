----------  *JAPANESE U'S*  ----------

The 3 U's that must be avoided or eliminated in the work area--

   1. Unreasonableness

   2. Unevenness

   3. Uselessness

Whenever anything unreasonable, uneven, or useless is observed,
it should be written down, and a remedy should be worked out
as soon as possible.


   Source--Total Quality Control (TQC): The Japanese Way.
           Q. C. Trends, ASugust 1985, page 9.
